Why Study DDoS Attack Types?

On Day 1, we looked at what a DDoS attack is and how botnets coordinate. Today, we dig deeper into the different types of DDoS attacks. Understanding these matters because our future log generator will need to capture the signatures of these attacks. Without that context, logs are just raw noise.
